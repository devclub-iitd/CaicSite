About

INAE

  • The Indian National Academy of Engineering (INAE) was founded in 1987 with the aim of promoting and advancing the practice of engineering and technology in the India.
  • It is an autonomous institution, registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860. It receives partial support via grant-in-aid programme of the Department of Science & Technology, Government of India. It is recognised as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation (SIRO) by the Department of Scientific and Industrial Research, Government of India.
  • The institute is a member of the International Council of Academies of Engineering and Technological Sciences (CAETS).

INAE Youth Conclave

  • INAE Youth Conclave aims at facilitating the engagement of Indian youth in engineering activities at national level.
  • INAE through the creation of Youth Forum desires to extend the membership to young budding engineers thereby endorsing their talent and advocating their ability to contribute meaningfully to the development of the country.
  • The First INAE Youth Conclave was organised at Birla Institute for Scientific Research (BISR), Jaipur on Aug 11-12, 2017. The Youth Forum was inaugurated by Dr. K Kasturirangan, FNAE, Chairman, Karnataka Knowledge Commission during the INAE Youth Conclave.

Themes

Health is Wealth

Bring the oft-repeated saying to life through a new, innovative idea or a scientific model. This case study is aimed at formulating a method to improve the general well being and health of people.

Digital Revolution

Think you have an idea or product that could change the digital landscape of the country? This case study requires you to present your idea/product in the technical field. What you choose to present is completely up to you.

Environment Protection

Bring the oft-repeated saying to life through a new, innovative idea or a scientific model. This case study is aimed at formulating a method to improve the general well being and health of people.

Lab to Market

Have an amazing idea or product that you’ve formulated, but not sure how to monetize it? Present your ideas at the Youth Conclave and get a platform to showcase your ideas and maybe get an opportunity to incubate your startup.

Waste to Wealth

377 Million Urban population,62 Million Tonnes of Waste generated. Present your ideas based on the implementation of sustainable, scientific and technological solutions for waste management.

The conclave aims to encourage and motivate our budding engineers to share their ideas and use it as a platform to showcase their innovations, allowing participants to come together from various disciplines and institutions to make a friendly network of young professionals. This rendezvous will act as a catalyst to motive our young entrepreneurs to materialize technical solutions to different problems and come up with business ideas. This conclave will also act as a platform to incubate ideas for different startups by energetic minds of young participants who love to take challenges and to see their dreams turning to reality.
